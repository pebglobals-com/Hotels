PEB Global Hotel

PEB Global Hotel is a 47-room hotel a few minutes away from Osubi Airport in Nigeria's Delta State.

The hotel offers a variety of room options, ranging from Studio Rooms to a self-catering suite, and has a restaurant and bar open seven days a week.

This brand-new property perfectly blends contemporary and traditional Nigerian themes and features a swimming pool, spa, gym and conference facilities, ideal for social gatherings or business functions.
